Interest in IT

I have been interested in IT and computers for as long as i can remember. As a kid I was fascinated by anything computerized,
Even the most basic computerized printer, Camera or Communicated device could hold my attention for longer that i care to admit.
Being in primary school during the 2000's, There was a lot of change In IT, And especial in regards to the internet
This sparked my interest in networking and what made this all work. We were taught on how to use the internet and this only furled my interest.
In high school i opted to do a lot of my work on computers, As such i got to know the IT admin well. He was the one that really got me thinking about a career in IT.

I chose RMIT as it offers a lot of flexibility in the courses, Which is great as due to my chronic insomnia my waking hours are erratic.
This allows me to study at my own pace. As a traditional uni requires you to study on campus. This would be impossible with my insomnia.
Studying with RMIT allows me to study at night, When there is not much to do. And allows me to be productive even when my sleep is not ideal.

I expect to learn not only The skills required to gain employment in IT. But but also gain other crucial skills in the process.
I found my typing skills have been lacking and this course gives me an opportunity to brush up on them.
My insomnia has made planning things a struggle. Studying at RMIT has giving me the opportunity to help me learn time management and planning skills.
